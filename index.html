Notes:

This schedule is carefully adjusted to avoid:

  1. Weekends (Fridays and Saturdays)
  2. Eid al-Adha holiday period (June 4-9, 2025)
  3. Other national holidays in Bangladesh

Daily breakdown of multi-day trainings:

  • ToT Batch 1: May 12 (Mon), 13 (Tue) = 2 days
  • ToT Batch 2: May 14 (Wed), 15 (Thu) = 2 days
  • FDM Training: May 18 (Sun), 19 (Mon), 20 (Tue), 21 (Wed), 22 (Thu) = 5 days
  • BOQ Training: July 13 (Sun), 14 (Mon), 15 (Tue), 16 (Wed) = 4 days
  • Care Point Training: August 10 (Sun), 11 (Mon), 12 (Tue), 13 (Wed), 14 (Thu), 17 (Sun), 18 (Mon) = 7 days

All trainings include Sundays as working days, but continue to avoid Fridays, Saturdays, and Bangladeshi holidays.
